### 🚀 About Me
I am a **Systems Engineering graduate from UNI**, specializing in **Software Development**. I am passionate about creating applications that provide a seamless, intuitive, and visually stunning user experience. I believe that great engineering should be felt in every navigation.

### 💡 Product, Data & Innovation Strategy
My engineering mindset is deeply rooted in the startup ecosystem and data-driven decision-making. My experience at **Yape** allowed me to bridge the gap between technical architecture and business viability, generating key insights through **SQL, Power BI, and Excel**. Furthermore, my participation in several **hackathons and startup incubation programs** has honed my ability to build high-impact MVPs, fostering a culture of rapid innovation and scalable product design.

### 🤖 The Path to AI & Fullstack Mastery
I am currently deep-diving into the modern web ecosystem (**Next.js, TypeScript, React, Node.js**) to build high-performance applications. My ultimate goal is to specialize in **LLMs** and **RAG** architectures, integrating intelligent layers into user-centric software.
